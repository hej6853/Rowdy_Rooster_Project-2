Overview

Dr. Hans Rosling explains in his book "Factfulness" that the majority of people perceive the world as more hopeless and negative than it really is.

We have picked the top 3 questions that have the most wrong answer rate to support the answer based on the data visualization as follows:

  • Research shows that the world is actually getting better, and showing a positive trend overall year-by-year.
  • This is in direct contrast to how even the top educated members of society view the overall trajectory of the world.
  • Understanding the true progress of humans and having the correct view of the progress of the world is important in government decisions regarding foreign aid.
