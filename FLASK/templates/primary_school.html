Q1. In all low-income countries across the world today, how many girls finish primary school?

Graph 1. Low-Income Countries Primay School Completion Rate (by gender)

**Low-income economies are those in which 2020 GNI per capita was $1,045 or less.

Low-Income countries' primary school completion rate 1970-2019
The official UN school data come from UNESCO, and the World Bank has calculated the number for so called “low income” countries, and it’s actually higher than what we just said: 63.2 percent in year 2015. (Reference: gapminder.org)




Graph 2. World Primay School Completion Rate (1970-2019)

Primary school completion rate worldwide 1970-2019
The completion rate of primary schools worldwide stood at around 90 percent in 2019. The completion rate increased over the past two decades as only about 82 percent of children in lower secondary age finished school in 2000. The highest completion rate was registered in 2016 when roughly 76.1 percent completed lower secondary school. (Reference: Statista)
